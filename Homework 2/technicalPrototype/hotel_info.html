Skopje Marriot Hotel

Get the celebrity treatment with world-class service at Skopje Marriott Hotel

Skopje Marriot Hotel is set in Skopje, 100 m from Macedonia Square and 200 from Stone Bridge. The hotel offers a spa centre with an indoor pool and facilities such as hot tub, massage and steam room.
Each room at the hotel features air conditioning, flat-screen TV with satellite channels, kettle and a safety box. A private bathroom is with free toiletries and hairdryer is also included.
A buffet breakfast is available every day at Skopje Marriot Hotel. Guests can also enjoy their meals at the property's restaurant serving Mediterranean and European cuisine.
You will find free WiFi and a 24-hour front desk at the hotel.
Kale Fortress is 800 m from the hotel and Skopje City Museum is 650 m away. The nearest airport is Skopje International Airport, 24 km from Skopje Marriott Hotel.
